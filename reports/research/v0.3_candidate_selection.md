# v0.3 Paper-Trading Candidate Selection

Decision date: `2026-06-13`

## Admission Rules

A strategy must satisfy all of the following:

1. Full child acceptance is `valid`.
2. Bias review has no blocking issue.
3. Net Sharpe is greater than the canonical rule-score reference.
4. Net total return is positive.
5. Required robustness evidence is complete and contains no skipped test.
6. A human-reviewable monthly approval packet can be produced.
7. No applicable data gap is classified as `blocks_paper_trading`.

`approved_for_paper_trading_candidate` is a research-governance state. It is not
approval for live trading, broker connectivity, or automated order routing.

## Outcome

No strategy is approved as `approved_for_paper_trading_candidate` in v0.3.

LightGBM is the primary rejected example: it has the strongest ML net Sharpe and
positive rank IC, but its `0.7778` net Sharpe remains below the rule reference
`1.1342`, with materially higher turnover.

The SEC text strategy is the second rejected example. Its small apparent Sharpe
improvement is not admissible because configured factor coverage failed acceptance.
Missing filing text is preserved as missing rather than treated as neutral.

The approved monthly packet proves the human approval mechanism can export candidate
weights. It does not override the strategy-level decision above.
